Our Services

Appraisals

Our licensed appraisal services include a variety of appraisals, virtual or on-site, focused on CMMI.

Evaluation Appraisal

Its purpose is to check progress toward a Benchmark Appraisal, check alignment of policies and processes with the CMMI model, and identify areas for improvement.

Benchmark Appraisal

Generates ratings using a Benchmark CMMI model view. Benchmark results are valid for a maximum of three years.

Sustainment Appraisal

Validates continuance of previous rating, it provides a two-year extension to the last appraisal result as long as the organization remains eligible.

Coaching

Our coaching services are related to business process improvement.

CMMI

The Capability Maturity Model Integration is an integrated set of best practices that enable businesses to improve performance of their key business processes.

Agile

Refers to a group of methodologies based on iterative development, where requirements and solutions evolve through collaboration between self-organizing cross-functional teams.

Six Sigma

It is a method that provides organization tools to improve the capability of their business processes thorugh statistical process control.
